JOLLOF RICE RECIPE

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish with many regional variations known for its vibrant flavors.
While the exact ingredients can vary across regions and individual preferences.Jollof rice can be a nutritious
dish when prepared with wholesome ingredients and enjoyed as part of a balanced diet. Here are some potential health
benefits associated with the key components of Jollof rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati or jasmine rice, rinsed well and drained
  • 1/2 cup vegetable or peanut oil
  • 1/2 medium red onion, sliced
  • 1 brocolli
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1 chicken or beef bouillon cube or 1 teaspoon bouillon powder
  • 3 pcs of garlic powder
  • 1 pc of Onion
  • 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 cup of green peas

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Prepare the pepper stew base Add the bell peppers, tomato, onion,
    and garlic into a blender, and blend until smooth. Set aside.
  3. Fry the onions for 1 minute to become little gold
  4. Cook the tomato paste about 3 minutes
  5. Cook the pepper stew base
  6. Add the rice and seasonings: stir rice with garlic,kosher salt
  7. Bake the jollof rice Turn the heat off and tightly cover the pot with foil and a lid.
    Transfer the pot into the preheated oven and bake, undisturbed, for about 40 minutes.
  8. Add butter and peas cover the pot for 5 minutes
  9. Serve: Gently stir the rice to incorporate the butter, peas, and onions.
