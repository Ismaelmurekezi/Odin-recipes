SPAGHETTI BOLOGNESE

This is one the best dish which is easy to cook

Spaghetti Bolognese is a popular Italian dish which can be a nutritious and balanced meal
when prepared with wholesome ingredients and enjoyed as part of a varied diet.
Apart from from being italic dish it is really liked by many people world wide

Spaghetti is a grain food. I recommends eating whole grains
because of their fiber content and nutritional benefit, have links
to a lower risk of heart disease, diabetes and cancer. Whole-wheat spaghetti is a whole-grain product and
has three additional grams of fiber per cup than regular spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 1Pc Pasta
  • 0.5kg Beef
  • 2pcs Tomatoes
  • 3pcs Garlic
  • 8 branches celery
  • 1pc Onions
  • 1pc Tomato paste
  • 1small cup Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Put 3 cups of water in sauce pan
  2. Prepare vegetable by slicing them for instance tomatoes,onions,celery,grind garlic after slice meat
  3. Put olive oil in another sauce pan
  4. Add beef in dish, stir it for 20 minute
  5. Add 4 cups of water of water in sauces pan
  6. After 30 minutes when meat remain with no water
  7. Add those vegetable in your dish
  8. after 8-10 minutes add those pasta in beef sauce pan
  9. stir it for 10 minutes
  10. There your dish is done.
